Antioxidants, Carotenoids, and Polyphenols

In recent years, antioxidants have gained considerable attention for their role in health and wellness. Among these, carotenoids and polyphenols stand out due to their extensive health benefits and natural sources.

What are Antioxidants?

Antioxidants are molecules that inhibit the oxidation of other molecules, thus preventing cellular damage. They play a crucial role in protecting the body from free radicals, which can cause various diseases.

Carotenoids

Carotenoids are pigments found in plants that are responsible for the red, yellow, and orange colors. They have been associated with a range of health benefits, including improved vision and reduced risk of certain cancers.

Polyphenols

Polyphenols are a category of compounds naturally found in plant foods, known for their antioxidant properties. They can help reduce inflammation and protect against chronic diseases.

Conclusion

Incorporating a variety of antioxidants, including carotenoids and polyphenols, into your diet can promote health and well-being. Foods rich in these compounds are essential for a balanced diet.
